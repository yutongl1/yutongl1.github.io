Final Results

Our first user said the Sunburst figure has too many layers, which actually district people. Then we changed our Sunburst figure into the second one, which excludes the species layer, and got good feedback from the second user. Thus, we tried the third one, which only includes kingdom, phylum, class, order, and family. But it is not informative enough. Thus, we chose the second one finally.

A more informative, interactive, beautiful and user-friendly website.
